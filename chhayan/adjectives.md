# Adjectives
Adjectives do not copy case, however gender and number does get copied.  
Endings:
| Masculine | Feminine | Neutral | Plural |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| -s | -a | -m | -i |

Example chart with `Ṕom`:
| Masculine | Feminine | Neutral | Plural |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `Ṕos` | `Ṕoa` | `Ṕom` | `Ṕoi` |
