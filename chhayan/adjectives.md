# Adjectives
Adjectives do not copy case, however gender does get copied.  
Endings:
| Masculine | Feminine | Neutral |
| --- | --- | --- |
| -s | -a | -m |

Example chart with `Ṕom`:
| Masculine | Feminine | Neutral |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `Ṕos` | `Ṕoa` | `Ṕom` |
