# Grammatical Gender
Grammatical gender is defined this way:  
| Standard ending | Gender |
| --- | --- |
| -a/-i | Feminine |
| -(consonant) | Masculine |

## Possesives
| English | Gender | Transliteration |
| --- | --- | --- |
| my | fem | mi |
| my | masc | man |
| my | plural | mais |
| your | fem | ti |
| your | masc | tan |
| your | plural | tais |
| our | fem | ni |
| our | masc | nan |
| our | plural | nais |
